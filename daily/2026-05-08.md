# Daily Research Radar — 2026-05-08

## Highlights

- **CFD surrogate의 무게중심이 “완전 대체”에서 solver-in-the-loop, stability, adjoint, safety filter로 이동 중이다.** FNO-LBM, ALE-consistent FSI, mesh temporal-awareness, buffet adjoint가 같은 방향을 가리킨다.
- **열/제조/건물 쪽은 foundation model보다 physics-informed architecture + calibration-free transfer가 핵심 신호다.** 주거건물 thermal foundation model과 composite 3D printing hybrid model이 좋은 응용 anchor다.
- **CAD automation은 데이터셋/표현이 병목이다.** CADFS와 Zero-to-CAD 모두 “예쁜 3D mesh”보다 executable CAD history / FeatureScript / editable construction sequence를 강조한다.
- **제어·안전 영역에서 neural operator가 실시간 제약 만족 도구로 들어오기 시작했다.** deformable media safety filter는 CFD/thermal design campaign의 safe exploration과 연결하기 좋다.

## Added Resources

### CFD surrogate, stability & solver-in-the-loop

- [Mesh Based Simulations with Spatial and Temporal awareness](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01542) — unstructured mesh learned simulation에서 node-wise supervision과 explicit Euler rollout의 한계를 지적하고 stencil-level objective, temporal correction, 3D RoPE를 제안. Code: <https://github.com/DonsetPG/graph-physics>.
- [An ALE-Consistent Graph Neural Operator-Transformer Framework for Fluid-Structure Interaction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00937) — moving/deforming mesh FSI에서 ALE-consistent boundary correction으로 long-rollout 안정성을 노리는 GNO+ViT surrogate.
- [Hybrid Fourier Neural Operator-Lattice Boltzmann Method](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27158) — FNO를 LBM 초기화/rollout coupling에 넣어 steady/unsteady weakly compressible flow를 가속. 순수 surrogate보다 “trusted solver 안에 ML 삽입” 패턴이라 실용성이 높다.
- [Large-eddy simulation nets based on physics-informed neural operator for wall-bounded turbulence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26621) — LES 방정식과 wall model을 PINO/F-FNO에 결합해 labeled data 의존을 줄이려는 wall-bounded turbulence surrogate.
- [Buffet Alleviation via Linear Stability Adjoint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04884) — transonic buffet onset을 empirical surrogate가 아니라 LST eigenvalue constraint와 coupled adjoint gradient로 shape optimization에 넣는 항공 CFD 최적화 논문.

### Thermal / manufacturing / control-oriented SciML

- [Toward a foundational thermal model for residential buildings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01364) — building-specific calibration 없이 다양한 건물/기후/제어전략으로 transfer하려는 physics-informed transformer thermal model.
- [Differentiable Multiphysics Co-Optimization via Implicit Neural Representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01040) — transient heat transfer, phase change, moving boundary, process control을 JAX solver + implicit neural geometry로 end-to-end co-optimization하는 benchmark.
- [Hybrid Machine Learning and Physical Modeling of Feedstock Deformation During Robotic 3D Printing of Continuous Fiber Thermoplastic Composites](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03186) — thermal stress, drying, crystallization, residual-stress relief를 Kelvin–Voigt + stabilized neural ODE로 묶은 manufacturing digital-twin example.
- [ZNO: Stable Rational Neural Operators in the Z-Domain for Discrete-Time Dynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02356) — discrete-time control/system ID에 맞춘 stable rational neural operator. near-unit-circle long-memory dynamics에서 해석 가능한 pole 구조가 장점.
- [Online Safety Filter for Deformable Object Manipulation with Horizon Agnostic Neural Operators](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01069) — PDE boundary input-output neural operator와 control barrier function/QP로 deformable/fluid manipulation safety constraint를 실시간 enforce.

### CAD, CAD automation & intelligent engineering workflows

- [CADFS: A Big CAD Program Dataset and Framework for Computer-Aided Design with Large Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01925) — 450k real-world CAD models를 FeatureScript executable program으로 재구성한 dataset/framework. Project: <https://voyleg.github.io/cadfs/>.
- [Zero-to-CAD](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24479) — LLM-in-the-loop CAD environment에서 executable/readable CAD sequences를 million-scale로 합성. CAD history가 있는 parametric data 확보가 핵심.
- [Awesome AI CAE](https://github.com/kimimgo/awesome-ai-cae) — CFD/FEA/SPH/DEM/differentiable simulation/neural operators/PINNs/MCP servers를 묶은 AI-ready CAE meta-index.
- [AeroJAX](https://github.com/arriemeijer-creator/AeroJAX) — JAX 기반 experimental differentiable aerodynamics project. real-time flow simulation, control, inverse design, neural-operator integration을 목표로 함.

## Best Find of the Day

**Hybrid Fourier Neural Operator–Lattice Boltzmann Method**가 오늘의 best pick. 이유는 단순히 “FNO가 빠르다”가 아니라, 작은 FNO가 순수 autoregressive rollout에서는 깨지지만 LBM과 coupling하면 error accumulation이 줄고 long-horizon 안정성이 좋아지는 패턴을 보여주기 때문이다. VA가 봐야 할 방향은 solver replacement가 아니라 **ML-as-accelerator inside validated CFD loops** 쪽이다.
